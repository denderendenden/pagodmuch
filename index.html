About Suisei

Hoshimachi Suisei (星街すいせい) is a Virtual YouTuber (VTuber) affiliated with Hololive Production. Known for her incredible singing talent and charismatic personality, Suisei has captivated audiences worldwide. She debuted independently in 2018 before joining Hololive, and has since become one of the most popular VTubers in the industry. Her dedication to music and entertainment has earned her a massive and devoted fanbase known as "Hoshiyomi" (星詠み).

Achievements

Suisei has achieved remarkable milestones throughout her career. She has held multiple sold-out solo concerts, including her legendary Nippon Budokan concert. Her original songs like "NEXT COLOR PLANET," "Stellar Stellar," and "BIBBIDIBA" have garnered millions of views on YouTube. She has also performed on THE FIRST TAKE, collaborated with major artists, and continues to push the boundaries of what a VTuber can achieve in the music industry.
